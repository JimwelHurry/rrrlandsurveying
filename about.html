Brief History

Precision Surveying with Expertise and Innovation

The firm was established in 2020 by Engr. Richard R. Rebueno, who managed to put up an office within a residential space while still working in construction industry. Engr. Rebueno, with his ample experience in land surveys and engineering surveys, started his small surveying firm and a surveying team of on-call workers with one set of total station equipment, a laptop and rented car for his service. Most of the firm's services were land surveying projects catered to clients within Rizal and nearby provinces.

In 2022 Engr.Rebueno decided to Leave the construction industry in which he has worked for almost a decade and gained most of his experience in construction surveys. Presently, the firm is located at #271 A. Mabini St. Brgy Hulo Pililia Rizal, its neighboring towns, and other areas within the region.
