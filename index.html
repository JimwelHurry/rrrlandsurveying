Our Services

Expert Geodetic Engineering Services for Accurate Surveys

Relocation Survey

We provide precise Relocation Surveys to ensure accurate boundary re-establishment and relocation of structures based on legal requirements and project needs.

Subdivision Survey

Our Subdivision Surveys offer detailed mapping and planning to guide the division of land into parcels, ensuring compliance with zoning and property regulations.

Original Survey

Our Original Surveys set the groundwork for land development, ensuring proper documentation of property boundaries and laying the foundation for legal property descriptions.

Resurvey

Our Resurveys re-evaluate existing land boundaries, providing updated and accurate data to resolve discrepancies or ensure compliance with new regulations.

Topographic Survey

We specialize in Topographic Surveys that provide detailed elevation and land contour data, essential for design and construction projects.

Hydrographic Survey

Our Hydrographic Surveys gather accurate data on bodies of water, including depths, shoreline boundaries, and underwater features, crucial for waterfront developments and navigation.

Construction Survey

We offer Construction Surveys to guide the layout of structures, ensuring precise placement of buildings and infrastructure in alignment with project plans.

As-Built Survey

Our As-Built Surveys provide detailed documentation of construction projects after completion, highlighting deviations from original plans and ensuring compliance.

Sketch Plan

We prepare Sketch Plans that serve as a visual representation of land features and proposed changes, useful for initial planning and approvals.

Subdivision Scheme

Our Subdivision Scheme services assist in designing and mapping out subdivisions, ensuring adherence to legal requirements and zoning regulations.

Location & Vicinity Map Plan

We create Location & Vicinity Map Plans that clearly outline a property’s location within its surrounding area, providing essential information for permits and legal documentation.
